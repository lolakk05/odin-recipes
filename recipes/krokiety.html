Krokiety

Author: Przepisy.pl

Ingredients

  • broth meat 0.5 kilograms
  • Knorr chicken broth 1 piece
  • Pre-soaked mushrooms 5 pieces
  • white onion 2 pieces
  • eggs 2 pieces
  • milk 1 cup
  • flour 100 grams
  • salt oil 6 spoons

Prepare step by step

Step 1

Slice the onions into feathers. Heat two tablespoons of oil in a pan.

Step 2

Saut√© onion, chopped mushrooms and boneless broth meat. Add a cube of Knorr chicken stock and stir until thoroughly dissolved. Stew the meat for a while. Then grind the whole thing in a grinder until smooth.

Step 3

Prepare the pancake batter. Mix the milk with the eggs, two tablespoons of oil, salt and flour.

Step 4

Fry thin pancakes. Then put a portion of meat on each and roll up croquettes.

Step 5

Fry the ready-made croquettes in the remaining oil so that the pancakes become slightly crispy. Serve immediately.
